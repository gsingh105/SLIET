Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology offers B.E courses at the UG level, MBA, M.Tech, and M.Sc at the PG level, and Ph.D. courses. It also offers diploma and certificate courses. Admissions to all these courses are entrance based. SLIET also conducts its exam, known as the SET EXAM for (admissions to certain courses). SLIET is a premier institution that takes an affordable and nominal fee and imparts priceless education to its students. It offers B.E. courses in various departments like computer science, electrical, electronics, food, mechanical, chemical and instrumentation engineering. Not only that it also offers honors in welding which plays an important and major role in today's mechanical field. The most profound and appreciated part of our college is that it consists of a well and highly qualified faculty with vast teaching experience. It also offers the provision of various Scholarships & Tuition Fee waivers (TFW) for needy meritorious students.

Vision & Mission

"To build a rich intellectual potential embedded with inter-disciplinary knowledge, human values and professional ethics among the youth, aspirant of becoming engineers and technologists, so that they contribute to society and create a niche for a successful career." Our college is a vibrant and inclusive community that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. Our students come from diverse backgrounds and cultures, and they are all committed to learning, innovation, and positive change. "Together We can make a Difference!"


Training and Placement

As SLIET offers a bachelor's degree in fields ranging from Computer Science, Electronics & Communication to Chemical Engineering and Food Technology, correspondingly it captivates corporate firms which are well renowned and promising for their great supportive and positive work environment to the employees. 90+ firms, videlicet Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, Infosys, IBM, Walmart, Mahindra, Maruti Suzuki, Hindustan Unilever Ltd., Reliance, Cognizant, Capgemini, and many more visit the institute campus every year for the recruitment of these proficient and skilled students. The graph of students' placement records has witnessed an exponential leap in both qualitative and quantitative aspects. For the record, in the academic session 2021-22, the highest package offered was INR 18 lacs while the median range of packages was around INR 4.10 lacs. An increase of nearly 14% has been observed in the medium package since the previous year. More than 100 companies visited and 350 offers were made during 2022 placements. Above 70% of the no. of total students from all the branches got placed.
